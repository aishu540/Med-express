Compliance & Legal Information

We strictly adhere to the guidelines of

  • U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)
  • Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA)
  • Customs and Border Protection (CBP)
  • Health and Human Services (HHS)

Only licensed pharmacies and healthcare providers can legally ship medicines. MedExpress does not buy or sell medications — we only facilitate secure and compliant delivery based on your valid prescription.

Your Questions, Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers about our medicine delivery process and international shipping guidelines. Can’t find what you need? Reach out to our support team below!

Can U.S. citizens receive medicines shipped from outside the U.S.?

Yes. U.S. citizens can receive medicines shipped internationally, subject to FDA and CBP regulations

Who can ship medicines through MedExpress?

Only authorized individuals and licensed pharmacies can use our platform for personal medicine shipping

Can I mail controlled substances?

No. Controlled substances like narcotics or steroids cannot be mailed internationally

Is the service legal?

Yes. We operate within the laws of India and comply with U.S. federal agencies (FDA, DEA, CBP)

Does MedExpress buy medications for me?

No. We only facilitate shipping. You or an authorized family member must purchase from a licensed pharmacy
